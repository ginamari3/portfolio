Project

L&L Logo Animation

  • Project Overview
  • Research & Discovery
  • Design Challenge
  • Process & Outcome

Tools: After Effects, Illustrator

Duration: 3 weeks

Final logo animation of L&L Drive-Inn (1976 version).

1. Choosing the Logo

I decided to animate the classic L&L 1976 logo for its nostalgic value and strong brand presence in Hawai‘i.

2. Choosing Local Brands

We were asked to explore local Hawai‘i-based brands. I considered a few but ultimately chose L&L for its simplicity and local identity.

3. Creating a Moodboard

I gathered imagery that captured the flow, movement, and vibe I wanted — such as warm color palettes, island textures, and retro type treatments.

4. Reference Images

I collected visual references to establish the animation style and energy. These helped guide the rhythm and shape transitions.

5. Storyboard

Before animating, I sketched a rough storyboard to visualize motion sequences and transitions.

6. Style Frames

I created style frames in Illustrator to guide the look and feel of key animation moments.

7. Sketchbook Concepts

Initial ideas were developed in my sketchbook as thumbnail frames and layout experiments.
