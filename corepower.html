Competitive Research

YogaWorks

  • Focus: Traditional yoga practices with strong teacher training programs.
  • Experience: Studio-based classes with a reputation for skilled instructors.
  • Weakness: Less emphasis on modern fitness or hybrid styles.

Pure Yoga

  • Focus: Luxury yoga experience with spa-like facilities and a variety of yoga styles.
  • Experience: Premium memberships, often located in major metropolitan areas.
  • Weakness: Less accessible and affordable for the average user.

Equinox

  • Focus: High-end fitness club offering yoga alongside strength and cardio training.
  • Experience: Emphasizes overall fitness with yoga as one component.
  • Weakness: Yoga experience may feel secondary or less community-driven.
